How much does it cost to build a website?

The cost of building a website varies depending on the features you need and whether you opt to build it yourself with a website builder or pay a developer to build it for you. On a website builder such as Wix, you can build as many websites as you want for free, however, you’ll have to upgrade to a Premium plan to connect a custom domain and get advanced business features. The cost of building a website may be significantly higher if you don’t opt for an all-inclusive platform like Wix.
